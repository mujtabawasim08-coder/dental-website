General Oral Health Tips

A healthy smile starts with simple, consistent habits. Incorporating daily care routines can prevent dental issues and improve overall wellness.

  • Brush your teeth twice daily using fluoride toothpaste.
  • Floss to remove plaque from areas your brush can't reach.
  • Use an antiseptic mouthwash to control bacterial growth.
  • Reduce sugary and acidic foods to prevent enamel erosion.
  • Schedule dental check-ups every six months.
  • Drink plenty of water to maintain saliva flow.
  • Replace your toothbrush every 3 months.

Common Dental Problems

Recognizing dental issues early allows for timely intervention and avoids costly treatments.

  • Cavities: Tooth decay caused by sugar and plaque.
  • Gum Disease: Gingivitis and periodontitis.
  • Tooth Sensitivity: Due to enamel loss.
  • Bad Breath: From bacteria or gum disease.
  • Discoloration: Coffee, tea, smoking.
  • Broken Teeth: Accidents or grinding.

Preventive Dental Care

Prevention is better than cure!

  • Routine dental check-ups.
  • Professional teeth cleaning.
  • Fluoride treatments.
  • Dental sealants.
  • Custom mouthguards.
  • Oral cancer screenings.

Daily Oral Care Routine

Consistency keeps your smile healthy.

  1. Brush twice daily for 2 minutes.
  2. Floss every night.
  3. Clean your tongue.
  4. Use mouthwash.
  5. Eat a balanced diet.
  6. Drink plenty of water.

Children & Adult Dental Care

Dental needs change with age.

  • Teach brushing habits early.
  • Limit sugary snacks.
  • Adults manage gum health.
  • Seniors need regular check-ups.
  • Use age-appropriate fluoride.

Oral Health & Overall Health

Your mouth affects your whole body.

  • Healthy gums protect the heart.
  • Good hygiene improves digestion.
  • Prevents infections.
  • Boosts confidence.
